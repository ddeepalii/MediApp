About Us

MediApp is a comprehensive digital platform designed to streamline the management and maintenance of medical equipment across healthcare institutions. We bring together hospitals, technicians, and suppliers in a unified ecosystem to ensure seamless operations, timely maintenance, and efficient procurement of medical devices.

Our mission is to enhance healthcare delivery by minimizing equipment downtime, optimizing maintenance workflows, and ensuring that hospitals have access to the right tools at the right time.

  • Hospitals: Monitor equipment status, request maintenance, and manage orders effortlessly.
  • Technicians: Receive alerts, manage service schedules, and update maintenance logs.
  • Suppliers: Respond to hospital needs, manage inventory, and fulfill orders efficiently.

At MediApp, we believe that technology can transform healthcare infrastructure. By bridging the gap between service providers and healthcare institutions, we aim to create a smarter, more responsive medical ecosystem.
